## 1. INTELLIGENCE SUMMARY — The "Soul" of Monroe Bay

**Owner/Winemaker:** Kiki Apple (Kirsten) & Walter Lamore
**Established:** Nov 15, 2014 | Farm Winery License
**Location:** 4786 James Monroe Highway, Colonial Beach, VA 22443 (barn) + 10 Washington Ave (original tasting room)
**Acreage:** 75 waterfront acres on the James Monroe Birthplace Farm
**Phone:** (804) 410-5628
**Hours:** Sat–Sun 12–5pm, Summer Fri 5–8pm | April–December (closed Jan–Mar)
**Tasting Fee:** $9 | Bottles: $18–$24
**Wine Trail:** Chesapeake Bay Wine Trail

### The Origin Story
Kiki Apple fell in love with winemaking at the West Mill House in the South of France (which she still owns). Back in Northern Virginia, she started making wine in her garage, then planted vines in Goldvein, VA. In 2014, she and Walter Lamore secured the license on 75 acres of the James Monroe Birthplace Farm — land where America's 5th president was born in 1758.

### The Strawbale Barn (opened July 2019)
- 3,000 sq ft, air-conditioned
- Straw bale insulation (R-30+ natural)
- Hydraulic lime walls with museum-quality white finish
- 10,000+ recycled wine bottles forming stained-glass wall patterns
- Lofty wood-paneled ceiling
- Bottle return program: customers bring empties to be mortared into future walls

### The Portfolio
**Reds:** Cabernet Franc, Petit Verdot, Chambourcin, Blue Heron Red
**Whites:** Viognier, Chardonnay, Petit Manseng, Tramminette, Pinot Grigio, Blue Heron White
**Rosé:** Blue Heron Rosé
**Ciders:** Boardwalk, Hard Apple (Hughes Crab apples — the variety Monroe grew), Hopped, Pumpkin Spice
**Non-Alcoholic:** Sparkling Red & White Grape Juices

### Blue Heron Labels
Designed by local artist Barbara Brennan (won annual label competition). Bright blues and greens, all featuring a Blue Heron — the sentinel bird of Monroe Creek.

### Wedding Venue
- 100–200 seated inside the barn
- 400+ ft of secluded waterfront
- 6 manicured acres for outdoor ceremonies
- 4.9/5 rating on WeddingWire (99% recommended)
- Flexible catering, food truck welcome, pet-friendly

### Events
- "A Brush with Wine" art classes (NNArtists.com)
- Stone Room Concerts (acoustic music)
- Christmas Bazaar
- Swing Dances
- Harvest & Crush volunteer days
- Food Truck Fridays (summer)

### Vibe
"Relaxed, Casual, Blue Heron/Jimmy Buffet" — Adirondack chairs, pets welcome, bring a picnic, waterfront sunsets, food trucks.

### 5C. UX Audit — 5 Friction Points on Current Site (monroebaywine.com)

1. **Site is DOWN** (ECONNREFUSED) — The current site returns connection errors. Zero revenue or discovery is happening digitally. Our rebuild eliminates this entirely.
2. **No CTA above the fold** — The current/cached site had no booking, reservation, or email capture above the fold. New design has "Reserve a Tasting" in hero, nav, and footer.
3. **No wine detail pages** — Wines were listed as simple text with no tasting notes, pairings, or pricing. New design includes filterable wine cards with expandable tasting notes and pairing suggestions.
4. **No wedding lead capture** — Wedding info was a text-only page with no form. New design has dedicated wedding page with embedded form + 4.9 rating badge for social proof.
5. **No mobile optimization** — Current/cached site was a 2010s-era layout not responsive. New design is mobile-first with responsive breakpoints at 640px, 768px, and 1024px.
